List of knowledge models and when to use them

Knowledge models are questionnaire templates, they decide what questions will be asked, and need to be selected when creating a new project. In order to obtain a document file with the questions and answers, an output template has to be selected as well (this is done in Settings tab, inside the project). The table below lists the knowledge models available at dsw.scilifelab.se, their purpose, and which templates are suitable to use with them.

Note: The knowledge models in bold are the most likely ones to be useful.

Name When use? Recommended template(s)
SciLifeLab Science Europe / VR DMP Suitable when writing a full DMP, compliant with Vetenskapsrådets template. Can also be used, with the SNIC-SENS tag, for the abridged DMP required when applying for a project at Uppmax Bianca. SciLifeLab Vetenskapsrådet DMP, SciLifeLab Full, SciLifeLab Minimal, SciLifeLab-SNIC-SENS
NBIS Support Checklist Used in NBIS Support projects to evaluate data management needs in the project. Support checklist, Support checklist Phase1, Support checklist Phase2
Tryggve checklist on ELSI issues and GDPR compliance Checklist to ensure that ethical and legal requirements are met in collaboration projects across borders. SciLifeLab Full, SciLifeLab Minimal
Common DSW Knowledge Model Full data management model, for training. Questionnaire Report
Life Sciences DSW Knowledge Model Life sciences customization of full data management model, for training or for Science Europe or Horizon projects. Questionnaire Report, Horizon 2020 DMP, maDMP, Science Europe DMP Template
FAIR Convergence Matrix Questionnaire Testing and training of FAIR Implementation Profiles for communities. Questionnaire Report
mini-Science-Europe from dsw lifescience For development only. Contains only questions with Science Europe DMP tags in the Life Sciences DSW knowledge model. -
