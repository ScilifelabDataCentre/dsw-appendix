Data Stewardship Wizard

Instructions how to start writing a DMP

1. Click on Questionnaires in left-side menu

2. Click on Create

In the top right corner.

  • Write a Name based on your name and project
  • Select "Common SciLifeLab DMP 1.1.0" (or the highest version number) as Knowledge Model
  • Click on Save and begin answering the questions

3. Export

After saving your answers, you can export the DMP



There are currently three export templates, SciLifelab Minimal reduce output somewhat, the focus is the questions and answers.
