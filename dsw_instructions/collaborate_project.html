How to share and collaborate on a project

There might be situations where you might want to share a link to your project:

  • Collaborators in the project needs to answer some questions
  • You want a stakeholder, such as the NBIS data steward or a SNIC center, to be able to view the answers
In order to make this possible, click on Share in the top right corner of the navigation bar.



Activate the Public link and select if you want the person with the link to view or edit, click on Save.



Now you can copy the link in the address bar (e.g. YvonneKallberg_VR-Project_v1) and share with whomever you want, without them having to log in!
